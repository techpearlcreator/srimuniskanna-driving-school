FAQ

1) How many driving lessons do I need before taking my driving test?

The number of lessons varies for each person, but most students need around 20 to 30 lessons to feel confident and test-ready. Your instructor will guide you based on your progress.

2) What documents do I need to bring for my driving test?

You’ll need to bring your learner’s permit, a valid form of identification, and any required test fees. Check with your instructor for any additional requirements.

3) What happens if I fail my driving test?

If you fail, don’t worry! Your instructor will help you understand your mistakes and work with you to improve. You can usually book a retest after a short waiting period.

4) Do you offer both manual and automatic driving lessons?

Yes, we offer both manual and automatic driving lessons. You can choose based on your preference or future driving needs.

5) How long does it take to get a driving license after passing the test?

It usually takes about 2 to 4 weeks to receive your driving license by mail, depending on the local transport authority's processing time.
